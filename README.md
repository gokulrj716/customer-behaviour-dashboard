Customer Behaviour Analytics Report
Project Overview

This report presents the analysis of customer behaviour data collected from 1,000 customer records. The objective of this project was to analyze customer purchasing patterns, engagement behaviour, spending habits, and satisfaction levels to generate meaningful business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

The dataset was processed, cleaned, analyzed, and visualized through an interactive dashboard to provide a clear understanding of customer trends and business performance.

Objectives of the Analysis

The primary objectives of this analysis were:

To understand customer spending behaviour
To identify high-value customer segments
To analyze customer satisfaction and engagement
To evaluate preferred payment methods and shopping channels
To study revenue contribution across cities and product categories
To generate actionable insights for improving business strategies
Data Preparation & Processing
Data Cleaning Performed

The dataset underwent several preprocessing steps to ensure data accuracy and reliability:

Removed duplicate records
Handled missing and null values
Corrected inconsistent data entries
Standardized categorical values
Validated numerical fields
Structured data for analysis and dashboard visualization

Dashboard Analysis Summary
1. Customer Segmentation Analysis

Customers were categorized into four major segments:

Segment	Observation
Regular Customers	Largest customer group contributing consistent revenue
Premium Customers	Highest spending customers with strong engagement
Occasional Customers	Moderate contribution with irregular purchases
Churned Customers	Lowest engagement and satisfaction levels
Key Insight

Premium customers contribute significantly higher revenue compared to other segments and should be prioritized for retention strategies.

2. Revenue Analysis
Total Revenue
Total revenue generated: ₹1.37 Crore
Average Spend Per Customer
Average customer spending: ₹13,658
Observation

Premium customers recorded the highest average spending, indicating strong purchasing power and brand loyalty.

3. Customer Satisfaction Analysis
Average Satisfaction Rating
Overall customer satisfaction: 3.50 / 5
Segment-Based Satisfaction
Segment	Rating
Premium	Highest satisfaction
Regular	Good satisfaction
Occasional	Average satisfaction
Churned	Lowest satisfaction
Key Insight

Lower satisfaction among churned customers indicates a need for improved customer retention and engagement strategies.

4. Payment Method Analysis
Most Preferred Payment Methods
UPI was the most frequently used payment method
Credit and Debit cards followed closely
Key Insight

Customers demonstrate strong preference toward digital payment systems, highlighting the importance of seamless online payment experiences.

5. Shopping Channel Analysis
Preferred Customer Channels
Mobile App usage dominated customer purchases
Website purchases were the second most preferred channel
In-store customers showed higher spending per transaction
Key Insight

Mobile platforms play a major role in customer engagement and should remain a strategic focus area.

6. Age Group Analysis
Largest Customer Group

Key Insight

Senior customer groups represent a valuable market segment and may require targeted retention and loyalty programs.

Business Insights & Recommendations
Key Business Insights
Premium customers generate substantially higher revenue
Digital payment adoption is strong among customers
Mobile applications drive the majority of engagement
Customer satisfaction directly impacts retention
Certain cities and age groups offer strong growth potential
Recommendations
Implement loyalty programs for premium customers
Improve engagement strategies for churned customers
Enhance mobile app experience and digital payment integration
Develop targeted marketing campaigns for high-performing cities
Increase customer satisfaction initiatives to reduce churn
Conclusion

The analysis successfully transformed raw customer data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, SQL analysis, Excel processing, and Power BI dashboard visualization.

The dashboard provides an effective overview of customer behaviour, revenue trends, satisfaction metrics, and engagement patterns, enabling better strategic decision-making and business growth opportunities.

This project demonstrates the practical application of data analytics, business intelligence, and visualization techniques in solving real-world business problems.
